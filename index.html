Features

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

GDPR Privacy First

Your CV data is stored only in your browser session. When generating a PDF, data is sent transiently to our EU rendering server and never stored or shared. No account, no email required.

ATS-Optimized Output

All three templates are structured so Applicant Tracking Systems can read every word. No tables, no text boxes, no parsing errors.

Country-Aware Format

German employers expect a photo. British ones don't. French CVs need a competences column. We handle all of it automatically.

Instant Live Preview

See your CV update in real time as you type. No saving, no refreshing. What you see is exactly what you download.

Style Customisation

Choose from 8 accent colours and 3 heading fonts. Every combination looks professional and print-ready on A4.

Completely Free

No trial period. No premium tier. No watermarks. europin.org is free for everyone, supported only by non-intrusive Google Ads.

FAQ

Questions Answered

Is europin.org really free?▾

Yes — completely free. No premium tier, no watermarks, no credit card. The site is supported by non-intrusive Google Ads which appear discreetly on the page.

Do I need to create an account?▾

No. Your data stays in your browser session. We never ask for your email, name, or any personal information to use the builder.

Is my data GDPR-compliant?▾

Yes. Your CV data is stored only in your browser session. When generating a PDF, it is sent transiently to our EU rendering server solely to produce the PDF file — it is never stored or shared. Close the tab and your data is gone. We comply with GDPR Articles 17 and 20.

Which countries are supported?▾

Our templates are optimized for Germany, Austria, Switzerland (Berlin), France and Francophone Belgium (Paris), and the UK, Ireland, and Commonwealth (London). More country templates are coming.

Will the PDF pass ATS scanners?▾

Yes. Our PDF output is text-based with clean HTML structure — no images of text, no tables, no text boxes. All major ATS systems (Workday, Taleo, Greenhouse, Lever) can parse it correctly.

Can I upload a photo?▾

Yes, for the Berlin and Paris templates which expect a professional photo. Our built-in crop tool lets you position and zoom your photo to a perfect circle before it's placed on the CV.

Can I edit my CV after downloading?▾

Your session data persists while your browser tab is open. Come back to it, make changes, and re-download. We recommend keeping the builder tab open until you're happy with the result.

What languages are supported?▾

The builder interface is in English but you can fill in all fields in any language. The CV itself will be in whatever language you type — German, French, Spanish, Dutch, Polish, etc.
