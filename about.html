Our Story

Built for European Job Seekers

europin.org is a free, privacy-first CV builder designed for the realities of the European job market — where a Lebenslauf in Germany, a CV in France, and a résumé in the UK are three entirely different documents.

Mission

What We Do

Finding a job is hard enough without spending hours wrestling with CV formatting rules that differ by country. We built europin.org so that anyone — whether you're a recent graduate in Madrid, a professional relocating to Munich, or a developer applying to London tech firms — can create a polished, correctly formatted CV in minutes, for free, with no account required.

Every template we offer is researched against what real recruiters and ATS systems in each country actually expect. The Berlin template follows German Lebenslauf conventions including the optional professional photo. The Paris template uses the two-column layout preferred by French HR departments. The London template applies the clean, concise style that British recruiters look for.

All of this is free. No watermarks. No hidden paywalls. No data harvesting.
